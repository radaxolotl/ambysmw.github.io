Amby in Super Mario World is a ROM hack of the original Super Mario World game, replacing the graphics of Mario with Amby the axolotl.
You can play it on just about any SNES emulator (I recommend SNES9x) by patching the IPS file with an IPS patcher (like Lunar IPS) to a Super Mario World ROM file.



Budgie in Super Mario Bros. is a ROM hack of the original Super Mario Bros. game, replacing the graphics of Mario with Budgie the parakeet.
You can play it on just about any NES emulator (I recommend FCEUX) by patching the IPS file with an IPS patcher (like Lunar IPS) to a Super Mario Bros. ROM file.
